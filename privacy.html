Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy page of the Blood Bank Management System. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as name, contact information, blood group, and donation history to manage and facilitate blood donations.

How We Use Your Information

  • To maintain donor records and facilitate blood donation requests
  • To notify donors about upcoming donation opportunities
  • To ensure the safety and availability of blood supply

Data Security

We take security measures to protect donor and recipient data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Sharing of Information

Your data will not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law or for emergency medical purposes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us.
